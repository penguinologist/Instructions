To convey ideas, nothing beats a well prepared presentation. LibreOffice Impress can help.

Pre-requisites

  • An installation of LibreOffice
  • Text to be entered
  • Time

Steps

  1. Open LibreOffice Impress
  2. Proceed to enter a title in the title field and other text where you wish.
  3. Creating new slides can be done by right-clicking the left bar and selecting "New slide" for as many slides as preferred.
  4. The background and other personalization can be done using the toolbar on the right.
  5. When done, click the File menu in the top left corner
  6. Click save
  7. Proceed to pick a place where to save and give the document a name.
  8. Click save
  9. Finished
