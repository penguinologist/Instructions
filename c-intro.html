Downloading and installing LibreOffice

This is a step by step guide to installing LibreOffice:

  1. Head over to LibreOffice and click the "Download Now" button
  2. Click the "Download version #.#.#" button to download the main installer.
  3. Wait for the file to finish downloading. This may take a few minutes depending on your internet connection speed.
  4. Open the installer by clicking it. When asked to run the file, click "Run".
  5. Click "Next" on the first page of the Installation Wizard.
  6. Click "Next" on the second page of the Installation Wizard to install a typical installation.
  7. Click "Install" on the third page of the Installation Wizard after checking the boxes for the functions you wish enabled.
  8. Wait for the process to finish. This may take several minutes.
  9. Once completed, click "Finish" to end the installation and exit the installer.
