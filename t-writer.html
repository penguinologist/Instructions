One of LibreOffice's main functionalities is the creation of documents. This is done in the Writer program in the LibreOffice package.

Pre-requisites

  • An installation of LibreOffice
  • Time to write a letter
  • A creative mindset

Steps

  1. Open LibreOffice Office
  2. Enter text as desired
  3. Modify fonts by selecting it out of the dropdown menu
  4. Click the File menu in the top left corner
  5. Click save
  6. Proceed to pick a place where to save and give the document a name.
  7. Click save
  8. Finished
