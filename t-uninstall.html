If for some reason you wish to uninstall LibreOffice, proceed as follows.

Steps

  1. Open the start menu and head to the control panel
  2. Once there, navigate to Programs, and then to Programs and features, seeking to uninstall a program.
  3. Click once on LibreOffice in the list to select it.
  4. Click "Uninstall" in the top bar.
  5. Wait for the process to finish.
  6. All done! You're finished
